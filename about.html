Andrés Orihuela Zarazua

My name is Andrés. I am an architecture graduate from the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) in Mexico City, currently based in Adelaide, Australia. My academic background has been shaped by experiences in both Mexico and Germany.

My work explores architecture as a cultural and social practice — balancing conceptual thinking with practical implementation. I am drawn to adaptive reuse, boutique residential and interior architecture, where spatial sensitivity and material precision shape meaningful environments.

Through my academic work, I developed experience engaging with broader urban and community contexts, enabling me to approach projects with an understanding of architecture across multiple scales.
