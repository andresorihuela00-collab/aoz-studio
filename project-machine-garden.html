La Máquina en el Jardín

A regenerative architectural thesis that reimagines Bordo Poniente, a former landfill in Mexico City, as a landscape of ecological restoration and community empowerment. Through industrial symbiosis, the project transforms waste into value: biocarbon becomes fertile soil, treated water sustains wetlands, and local flora supports a new medicinal economy. Combining research laboratories, hydroponic systems and spaces for collective healing, the project bridges infrastructure and well-being to envision a more just and resilient future.
