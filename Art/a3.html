Three Studies

Year 2026
Medium Acrylic on canvas (water-based)
Price 500 AUD

A triptych exploration of gesture, rhythm, and color. Each panel captures the same painterly movement in a different chromatic intensity—yellow, orange, and magenta with Brilliant Alizarin—creating a visual dialogue about energy, variation, and balance.
