Object Design — AOZ Studio

F1 Lounge Chair

A low lounge chair exploring the relationship between structure, material and craft. The woven leather surface creates a flexible seating plane suspended within a solid walnut frame, balancing comfort with architectural clarity.

Materials

  • Solid American Walnut
  • Vegetable-Tanned Leather
  • Solid Brass Hardware
  • Hand-Oiled Finish

Design Notes

Conceived as a continuous assembly of wood and leather rather than an upholstered object, F1 highlights the structural logic of each component. The woven seat and backrest age naturally with use, developing a unique patina over time.

Dimensions

W 760 mm  ·  D 820 mm  ·  H 710 mm

Designed by AOZ Studio  ·  Prototype 01  ·  2026
