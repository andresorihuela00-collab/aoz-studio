Viehmarkt Acting School — Reactivation of the old slaughterhouse of Munich

Viehmarkt Bank

This project stems from a deep commitment to preserving Munich's historical legacy and fostering a vibrant cultural and educational space. The decision to repurpose Viehmarkt Bank is grounded in its rich history and its contribution to Munich's culinary essence — preserving these 19th-century buildings is essential to maintaining ties with the past. It reimagines the historic Viehmarkt Bank as an acting school, honoring its educational past while contributing to the city's cultural fabric, transforming a former slaughterhouse precinct into a vibrant hub for culture, learning and collective interaction.
