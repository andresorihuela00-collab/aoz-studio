Nican Axcan — Here and now

MUNA

The museum, located in the Miguel Hidalgo borough near Coyoacán, seeks to provide its residents with an immersive experience in time. While exploring the four stages that most define the history of México, visitors will be able to understand and study the past in order to understand the present.

Coyoacán, Mexico City, Mexico
Year2022
ProgramMuseum
StatusAcademic Proposal

In collaboration with Mariel Ruiz and Ian Gerardo Flores

"We can't travel to the past, we can't travel to the future, so all the answers you need can be found in the Nican Axcan, in the here and now."
